Blinkit Clone UI

A pixel-perfect UI clone of the popular grocery delivery app, focusing on complex layouts and animations.

Project Overview

The objective of this project was to challenge my UI/UX development skills by faithfully recreating the interface of a popular, real-world application. I chose Blinkit for its dynamic layouts, custom iconography, and subtle micro-interactions. The focus was not on backend functionality, but purely on achieving a high-fidelity, performant, and responsive frontend that mirrors the production app.

Challenges & Solutions

One of the most complex parts to replicate was the "Add to Cart" button animation, which smoothly transforms from a simple button into a quantity selector. A simple state change was not enough. I solved this by using Flutter's `AnimatedSwitcher` and `AnimatedContainer` widgets. I wrapped the button states in a `StatefulWidget` and controlled the size, shape, and child widgets based on the cart quantity. This allowed for a fluid and satisfying micro-interaction identical to the original app.
